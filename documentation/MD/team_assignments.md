## 2. Detailed Group & Role Breakdown

### 👨‍💻 Anay Modi (Groups A & C)
* **Group A: System Administration & Control Center**
  1. **Airport Administrator:** Manages system configurations, master databases, and user access.
  2. **AOCC Director (Airport Operations Control Center):** Manages overall airport health, runway availability, and high-level flight schedules.
  3. **Gate & Stand Planner:** Assigns parking stands, terminals, and jet-bridges to arriving and departing flights.
  4. **Ground Operations Supervisor:** Overlooks the timeline of all turnaround tasks for parked aircraft.
* **Group C: Maintenance & Fueling Services**
  5. **Line Maintenance Engineer:** Conducts standard pre-flight inspections (tires, engines, structure).
  6. **Avionics Technician:** Inspects electronic systems, flight decks, radios, and autopilot sensors.
  7. **Aviation Refuelling Operator:** Connects fuel hoses to the aircraft wing and pumps fuel.
  8. **Fuel Safety Inspector:** Tests fuel density, quality, and approves refueling security.

---

### 📝 Anuvrat Tripathi (Groups B & E)
* **Group B: Ramp & Turnaround Operations (Ramp Side)**
  1. **Turnaround Coordinator (TCO / "Red Cap"):** Physically stands by the aircraft to coordinate all ground teams.
  2. **Aircraft Marshaller:** Guides the pilot to park the aircraft precisely at the assigned stand.
  3. **Pushback Tug Operator:** Handles pushback and towing of aircraft.
  4. **Baggage Loading Supervisor:** Directs the loading and unloading of checked passenger bags.
  5. **Cargo Handling Specialist:** Manages heavy commercial cargo and mail loaded in the aircraft holds.
* **Group E: Passenger & Terminal Services (Gate Side)**
  6. **Check-in Counter Agent:** Verifies passenger IDs, prints boarding passes, and tags bags.
  7. **Boarding Gate Agent:** Scans boarding passes, checks carry-on sizes, and manages passenger boarding.
  8. **Terminal Manager:** Oversees terminal conditions, security queues, and gate seating comfort.

---

### 💻 Krishna Solanki (Groups D & F)
* **Group D: Cabin & Catering Services (Aircraft Interior)**
  1. **Cabin Cleaning Supervisor:** Manages the cleaning crew and conducts quality inspections.
  2. **Cabin Cleaning Crew Member:** Physically vacuums, empties bins, cleans tray tables, and tidies seat pockets.
  3. **Catering Dispatcher:** Plans galley cart configurations and loads meals onto catering trucks.
  4. **Catering Loader:** Drives the high-lift truck, raises it to the galley door, and swaps out galley carts.
  5. **Water & Lavatory Service Operator:** Drains aircraft sewage tanks and refills clean, potable water.
* **Group F: Security, Regulatory & Emergency Teams**
  6. **Terminal Security Officer:** Conducts security screenings on passengers and cabin crew.
  7. **Aircraft Security Sweeper:** Conducts physical cabin security sweeps before passengers board.
  8. **Customs & Immigration Officer:** Manages visa checks, passport controls, and declaration forms for international flights.
  9. **Airport Emergency Services Liaison (Fire/Rescue):** Dispatches emergency response for fueling spills or technical fire alerts.
